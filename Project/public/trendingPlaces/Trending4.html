NEW DELHI

1. India Gate

A towering war memorial dedicated to Indian soldiers who died in World War I and the Third Anglo-Afghan War.Evening illuminations, Amar Jawan Jyoti (Eternal Flame), and lush lawns ideal for picnics.

2. Lotus Temple

A Bahá'í House of Worship known for its flower-like shape, attracting visitors with its serene ambiance and architectural beauty.Reflective pools, peaceful gardens, and meditation spaces.

TAJ MAHAL

3. The Taj Mahal, located in Agra, India, is one of the most iconic and beautiful monuments in the world. It is renowned for its stunning architecture, historical significance, and the romantic story behind its creation. It was built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan The Taj Mahal was built as a mausoleum for Shah Jahan’s beloved wife, Mumtaz Mahal.The Construction Period 1632 - 1653
