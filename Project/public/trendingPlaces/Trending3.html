PARIS

1. Eiffel Tower

The iconic symbol of Paris, offering breathtaking views of the city from its various levels.Observation decks on the second and third levels, dining at Le Jules Verne, and evening light shows.

2. Louvre Museum

The world's largest art museum, housing an extensive collection of art and artifacts from ancient civilizations to the 19th century.The Mona Lisa, Venus de Milo, Winged Victory of Samothrace, and the museum's stunning glass pyramid entrance.
