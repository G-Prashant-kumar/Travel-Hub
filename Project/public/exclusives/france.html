Discover France Tour

Embark on a 14-day luxury tour of France, exploring iconic cities, stunning landscapes, and historic landmarks. Enjoy gourmet dining, 4-star accommodations, and guided tours throughout your journey.

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Paris
Arrive at Paris Charles de Gaulle Airport and transfer to your hotel. Check-in and visit the Eiffel Tower, enjoy a Seine River cruise, explore the Latin Quarter, and dine at a local bistro.

Day 2: Paris
Visit the Louvre Museum, Notre-Dame Cathedral, Arc de Triomphe, and the Champs-Élysées.

Day 3: Paris
Visit the Palace of Versailles, enjoy a cabaret show at the Moulin Rouge, and explore the Marais neighborhood.

Day 4: Paris to Nice
Fly to Nice, visit the Promenade des Anglais, Old Town, and Cours Saleya Market.

Day 5: Nice
Day trip to Monaco, visit the Palace of Monaco, return to Nice for dinner and explore the Port of Nice.

Day 6: Nice
Visit the Chagall Museum, Castle Hill, and Cimiez Hill.

Day 7: Nice to Bordeaux
Fly to Bordeaux, visit the Place de la Bourse, Grand Théâtre, and Saint-Pierre district.

Day 8: Bordeaux
Take a wine tour, visit the Musée d'Aquitaine and the Cathedral of Saint Andrew.

Day 9: Bordeaux
Visit Arcachon Bay and enjoy a boat tour, return to Bordeaux and explore the Quays of Bordeaux.

Day 10: Bordeaux to Loire Valley
Drive to the Loire Valley, visit Château de Chambord and Château de Chenonceau.

Day 11: Loire Valley
Visit Château d'Amboise, enjoy lunch, explore Blois and visit Château de Blois.

Day 12: Loire Valley to Mont Saint-Michel
Drive to Mont Saint-Michel, visit the abbey, explore the bay, and enjoy dinner.

Day 13: Mont Saint-Michel
Take a guided tour of the abbey, explore Cancale, and enjoy the views of the bay.

Day 14: Departure from Paris
Drive back to Paris and depart from Paris Charles de Gaulle Airport.

Inclusions

Return airfare from your preferred airport, 13 nights' accommodation in 4-star hotels, daily breakfast and dinner, guided tours and activities, airport transfers and transportation between destinations.
