Discover Amsterdam Tour

Experience the best of Amsterdam with our 7-day luxury tour. Enjoy the city's iconic canals, world-renowned museums, and vibrant neighborhoods while staying in a 4-star hotel.

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Amsterdam
Arrive at Amsterdam Schiphol Airport and transfer to your hotel. Check-in and visit Dam Square, explore the surrounding streets, enjoy dinner at a local restaurant, and take a stroll along the canals.

Day 2: Amsterdam
Visit the Rijksmuseum, Van Gogh Museum, Anne Frank House, and take a canal boat tour.

Day 3: Amsterdam
Visit Vondelpark, explore the Jordaan neighborhood and Noordermarkt, and enjoy dinner with canal views.

Day 4: Amsterdam
Visit the Amsterdam Canal Ring, Museum Quarter, Stedelijk Museum, Heineken Experience, and take a bike tour.

Day 5: Amsterdam
Visit Zaanse Schans windmills, explore Haarlem, and enjoy dinner with canal views.

Day 6: Amsterdam
Visit the Amsterdam Flower Market, De Pijp neighborhood, Albert Cuyp Market, EYE Film Museum, and take a sunset cruise.

Day 7: Departure from Amsterdam
Spend the morning shopping for souvenirs or exploring the city before departing for the airport.

Inclusions

Return airfare from your preferred airport, 6 nights' accommodation in a 4-star hotel, daily breakfast and dinner, guided tours and activities, airport transfers and transportation between destinations.

Popular Attractions and Activities

Visiting coffee shops, exploring the Red Light District, visiting the Amsterdam Dungeon, taking a day trip to Utrecht, and visiting the Amsterdam Zoo.

Popular Restaurants and Cafes

De Kas (modern Dutch cuisine), Bauta (traditional Dutch stamppot), Cafe Papeneiland (coffee and pastries), De Foodhallen (international cuisine), The Flying Pan (breakfast pancakes).
