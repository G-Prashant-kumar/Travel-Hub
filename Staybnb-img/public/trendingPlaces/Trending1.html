DUBAI

1. Burj Khalifa

Standing at 828 meters, the Burj Khalifa is the tallest building in the world. It offers breathtaking views of the city from its observation decks on the 124th, 125th, and 148th floors. Visit during sunset for a mesmerizing view of the city transitioning from day to night.
